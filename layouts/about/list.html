Our Cooperative

FikaWorks is a collective of cloud-native engineers united by shared values and a commitment to technical excellence. As a cooperative, we're owned and democratically governed by our members—not external shareholders or investors. This structure ensures that decisions are made by those doing the work, keeping our focus on quality, collaboration, and long-term sustainability.

Unlike traditional IT consultancies organized in hierarchical structures, in our cooperative all members have an equal vote in deciding where the organization develops. We are fundamentally based on open source community practices—transparency, collaboration, and shared ownership define how we operate.

Collective Strength

Our extended network allows us to scale seamlessly, adapt quickly, and assemble the right team for every engagement. No matter the role, every FikaWorks professional operates under the same proven methodologies and shared values, ensuring consistent quality and results.

For our clients, this means access to a scalable, world-class talent pool that combines continuity, flexibility, and technical excellence.

Vision

We believe in staying small, grounded and focused on what matters: the work itself. Our cooperative exists to preserve the autonomy and independence that makes each member's practice distinctive, while providing the collective strength to tackle complex, meaningful projects.

No bureaucracy. No overhead. Just skilled professionals collaborating on projects they're genuinely interested in, supported by a lean cooperative structure that handles administrative tasks so members can focus on delivering exceptional technical work.

Unlike traditional consultancies where employees compete for advancement, we swarm and learn from each other. Knowledge is openly shared, not hoarded. Members collaborate across projects, bringing collective expertise to bear on complex challenges. This creates a culture of continuous learning and mutual growth—everyone gets better together.

This antifragile model doesn't just withstand challenges, it grows stronger from them. IT industry volatility, changing business needs, and technological shifts become opportunities for adaptation and growth.

Values

The cooperative is built around 4 core principles:

  • • Deliver value to clients through the use of open source (cloud-native) technologies
  • • Deliver value to members through collaboration, client pool sharing and co-branding
  • • Embed open source principles into our core operating model
  • • Build strong collective capability by accepting only senior, vetted consultants as partners
