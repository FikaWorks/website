At FikaWorks, we believe that **strong communities drive innovation**. As a collective of independent cloud-native engineers, we're deeply committed to building, supporting, and participating in the broader tech ecosystem.

We actively contribute to the growth of the cloud-native and AI ecosystem in the Netherlands and beyond. FikaWorks is a **founding member of [Cloud Native Netherlands Foundation](https://cloudnativenetherlands.nl/)**, an initiative dedicated to connecting cloud-native professionals and fostering knowledge sharing across the region.

## Supporting Community-Driven Conferences

We're passionate supporters of grassroots, community-driven conferences that bring together practitioners to share real-world experiences. Over time, we've proudly sponsored and supported events including:

- **Kubernetes Community Days (KCD)** - Utrecht, Amsterdam, and Romania
- **DevOps Days Amsterdam**
- **Kubetrain**
- **Kuberoke**
- **Cloud Native Rejekts Europe**

These events reflect our values: open collaboration, practical knowledge sharing, and building connections that matter.

## Active Organizers and Contributors

Our members don't just attend—they help shape these communities. FikaWorks members are actively involved in:

- **Organizing** Kubernetes Community Days, Cloud Native Days, Cloud Native Rejekts, and DevOps Days events
- **Reviewing talks** for KubeCon and other major conferences
- **Speaking and running workshops** at community events worldwide

## FikaWorks Day

Each year, we host **FikaWorks Day**—our own community event that brings together freelancers, consultants, entrepreneurs, and tech enthusiasts for a day of talks, workshops, and open collaboration. It's our way of giving back and creating space for the kind of meaningful conversations that drive our industry forward.
