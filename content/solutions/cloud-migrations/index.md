Cloud migration is one of the most consequential infrastructure decisions an organisation makes. Whether you are moving from on-premises to public cloud, consolidating multi-cloud sprawl, or migrating between providers, the stakes are high: downtime, data integrity, compliance, and cost are all on the line.

FikaWorks brings deep, hands-on experience across AWS, Azure, GCP, and Sovereign Neo-Cloud environments. We assess each workload individually, select the right migration pattern, and execute with the rigour of our proven 5-step process — from Discovery through Handoff. Infrastructure as Code is embedded by default: every migrated workload lands as reproducible, version-controlled infrastructure.
