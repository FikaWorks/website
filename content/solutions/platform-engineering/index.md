Platform engineering is the discipline of designing and building toolchains and workflows that enable self-service capabilities for software engineering organisations. Done well, it transforms how your teams ship software — replacing ticket-driven infrastructure requests with golden paths that are fast, safe, and consistent.

FikaWorks has been building cloud-native platforms since before "platform engineering" had a name. Our consultants have designed and operated Internal Developer Platforms for organisations ranging from startups to enterprises, across Kubernetes, serverless, and hybrid environments. Our blog post ["Accelerating Cloud-Native Adoption: A Platform Engineering Roadmap"](https://fika.works/blog/accelerating-cloud-native-adoption-platform-engineering-roadmap/) lays out the methodology.
