When your platform exists but nobody uses it, the problem is not the platform — it is the interface. Developers cannot adopt what they cannot find. A developer portal gives your organisation a single front door to services, documentation, scaffolding templates, and operational visibility.

FikaWorks deploys and customises Backstage-based developer portals that become the most-visited internal tool in your organisation. We do not just install software — we design the catalogue structure, integrate your existing toolchain, build the plugins that matter for your workflows, and run the adoption programme that gets developers using it.

**When you need more than a portal:** If your organisation needs the full Internal Developer Platform — golden paths, self-service provisioning, infrastructure automation — see our [Platform Engineering](/solutions/platform-engineering/) solution. The portal is one layer of a complete IDP.
