Individual AI agents solve individual problems. Agentic infrastructure lets you scale AI across your organisation — securely, observably, and sustainably. This means MCP servers that give agents governed access to your internal tools, pipeline orchestration that chains reasoning with action, and production-grade observability that keeps humans in control.

FikaWorks builds agentic infrastructure with the same engineering rigour we apply to cloud-native platforms. Every component is codified in Infrastructure as Code, observable through standard tooling, and operable by your team after handoff.
