The Discovery Workshop Service provides a structured, collaborative engagement
designed to capture a comprehensive understanding of your business objectives,
technical environment, and operational challenges. Through a series of
facilitated workshops and stakeholder interviews, FikaWorks consultants analyze
the current state, identify key opportunities, and define both strategic and
tactical priorities for transformation.

This service culminates in the delivery of a formal Strategy and Roadmap
Document, outlining clear recommendations, guiding principles, and prioritized
actions aligned with your short- and long-term goals. The outcome is a
validated, actionable plan that enables informed decision-making, accelerates
alignment across teams, and sets a strong foundation for successful platform
implementation and adoption.
